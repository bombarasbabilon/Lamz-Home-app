## Add to Home Screen

Follow these steps to install Health Tracker as a standalone app on your iPhone (just like Pocket Option):

### Step 1: Open in Safari
1. Open **Safari** browser on your iPhone
2. Navigate to your deployed app URL (e.g., `https://lamz-home-app.vercel.app`)

### Step 2: Add to Home Screen
1. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari
2. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Edit the name if desired (default: "Health")
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner

### Step 3: Launch the App
1. Find the **Health Tracker** icon on your home screen
2. Tap it to launch as a full-screen app
3. The app will run in standalone mode without Safari UI

## Troubleshooting

**App not installing?**
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Check that JavaScript is enabled
- Try closing other apps and restarting Safari

**Icon looks wrong?**
- Clear Safari cache and reinstall
- Make sure the PWA manifest is properly configured

**App not opening in full screen?**
- Delete the app from home screen
- Reinstall using the steps above
- Make sure you tapped "Add to Home Screen" (not "Bookmark")

## Requirements

- iOS 11.3 or later
- Safari browser
- Internet connection for initial install

## Privacy

All your data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers unless you explicitly export it.
